Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start or cancel my free trial?

Your 7-day free trial starts automatically when you subscribe from within the app. To cancel before you're charged, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone and cancel the Titra subscription at least 24 hours before the trial ends.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed entirely through your Apple ID. Open the Settings app, tap your name, then tap Subscriptions, and select Titra to change your plan or cancel.

Is my data private?

Yes. Titra is built with privacy in mind. See our full Privacy Policy for details on what's collected and how it's used.

Does Titra provide medical or dosing advice?

No. Titra is a personal tracking and organization tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or recommend dosages. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for guidance on your regimen.

I found a bug or have a feature request — what do I do?

Email support@titraapp.com with a description of what happened (and a screenshot if you have one). We read every message.
