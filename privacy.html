Titra Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Titra helps you track a peptide / GLP-1 protocol. Contact: micahforgey1@gmail.com

The short version

  • Your tracking data is stored on your device. We have no server that collects it.
  • We do not sell your data and we do not show ads.
  • The optional AI assistant sends limited data to a trusted provider to work (below).
  • Titra is a tracking tool, not medical advice. Follow your healthcare provider.

Data stored on your device

Everything you enter stays on your device: peptides, doses, schedules, injection sites, side effects, weights, goal weight, photos, and preferences. Deleting the app or using in-app Reset removes it.

Optional features that send data

If you use the assistant, your question and a short summary of your tracker data are sent to Anthropic (makers of Claude) through a secure server we operate, only to answer you.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never receive your payment details. Manage or cancel in your App Store settings.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or use third-party tracking.

Health data

What you track can be sensitive. You choose what to enter, it stays on your device unless you use the assistant, and you can delete it anytime. Titra does not diagnose or treat.

Children

Titra is for adults (18+) and is not directed to children.
